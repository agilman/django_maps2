Welcome to Django Maps

Django Maps is an open source, community website for travelers to document and share their adventures. Feel free to join our community on IRC. We would love to hear any questions, comments or suggestions!

Features

  • Mark your daily progress using interactive maps
  • Upload photos and mark their location
  • Keep a blog about your memorable moments
  • Compile a list of your gear
  • More features are in development
